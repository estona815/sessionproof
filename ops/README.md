# Private business evidence operations

Public templates are empty by design. Store receipts, contacts, messages, consent, and customer files only in `ops/private/`, which is gitignored, or in an approved private evidence store. Use anonymized IDs in public exports. Never fabricate or backdate evidence.

Reconcile monthly totals against provider statements, keep original currency and FX source, separate arms-length and related-party revenue, record refunds, and disclose marketing spend even when zero. Obtain customer awareness/consent before sharing evidence with judges.
