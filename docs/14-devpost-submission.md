# Devpost submission draft

**Title:** SESSIONPROOF — Evidence-Linked Music Credit Agent  
**Tagline:** From messy session notes to confirmed credits before release.  
**Category:** Entrepreneurship & Job Creation

Music credits often live across chats, spreadsheets, emails, invoices, and memory. SESSIONPROOF gives independent creators a focused pre-release workflow: submit only relevant records, extract supported contributor claims with Gemini, link every claim to an exact fragment, calculate deterministic conflicts, ask targeted questions, record private workflow acknowledgements, and export a review pack.

Gemini performs bounded structured extraction and question drafting. It cannot invent missing facts, total or repair splits, merge identities, decide ownership, or clear samples. The deployed service is designed for Cloud Run with Firestore, private Cloud Storage, Vertex AI Gemini, Secret Manager, and redacted Cloud Logging. Humans control source submission, identity merging, final values, contact, legal review, and release approval.

The working business model is a free fictional demo, a one-song paid Release Credit Check, and later a multi-release team pilot. The current pilot price is a hypothesis, not an industry benchmark. Actual customers, revenue, costs, and marketing spend must be inserted from verified ledgers before submission; they are currently zero.

Economic impact comes from helping independent creators preserve contributor work evidence and reducing the administrative cost of a professional release. A later human-review route could create scoped work for qualified music metadata and rights-administration specialists without pretending the AI is a lawyer or rights authority.

**Built with:** Next.js, TypeScript, React, Zod, Google Gen AI SDK, Cloud Run, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Secret Manager, Vertex AI Gemini, pdf-lib, Vitest, Playwright.
