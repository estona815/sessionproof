# Category strategy

Working category: **Entrepreneurship & Job Creation**.

SESSIONPROOF helps independent creators run a release as a small business: preserve work evidence, reduce missed professional credits, make contributor review more accessible, and create a structured handoff to music administration specialists when automation should stop. This category fit becomes credible only with real creator usage and economic evidence.

Alternatives: Small Business Services fits the operational product; Professional Services Access fits the future human-review referral. Entrepreneurship & Job Creation remains the clearest working choice because the primary user is an independent creator business and the output protects professional work evidence. Final selection requires interview evidence.
