# SESSIONPROOF — Evidence-Linked Music Credit Agent

SESSIONPROOF helps independent music creators turn scattered release records into a reviewable contributor ledger before release. Gemini extracts only source-supported candidates into a strict schema. Deterministic code calculates splits and detects contradictions. Humans approve identity mappings, final values, contact, legal review, and export.

The working business is a paid one-song Release Credit Check, with a free fictional Guided Demo and a later team workspace. Actual customer, revenue, cost, production, repository, URL, and video evidence must replace the clearly marked placeholders before submission.
